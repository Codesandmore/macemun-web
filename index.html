About

The Xth Anniversary Edition of the MACE Model United Nations (MACE MUN) Conference aims to scale higher and amalgamate a perfect blend of Diplomacy, Debating and Display of leadership skills at its finesse by endorsing the ability to APPREHEND the crises at hand, ACKNOWLEDGE everyone's stances, faults and solutions and ACCORD in goodwill. Hosted since 2014, the prestigious event simulates selected United Nations committees, each with designated country assignments. The Executive Board, comprising judges, determines the conference's agenda, addressing pressing global concerns that challenge peaceful coexistence.

Theme

Climate Justice: Global Call to Action

This pressing issue transcends geographical boundaries and impacts every corner of the planet. In an era defined by unprecedented environmental challenges, selecting "Climate Crisis" as the theme of an event underscores the gravity of the situation we face as global citizens. Climate change is no longer a distant threat; it's a stark reality shaping our daily lives. By making this the focal point of discussion and action, we acknowledge the urgency and importance of addressing the most significant crisis of our time. It calls for immediate action, unites diverse voices, and highlights our shared responsibility. This theme empowers individuals to advocate for change and emphasizes the need for sustainable solutions. By focusing on the "Climate Crisis," we commit to raising awareness and inspiring concrete actions for a sustainable future.
